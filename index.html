📖 How to Use This App

Society Attendance Portal — complete guide for admins and staff

👥 Two Roles — One App
🏢 Admin (Society Office)
1
Register once using Register Admin tab — enter your society name, email & password. This creates the office account.
2
After logging in, go to Manage Staff tab → fill in each staff member's name, their email (the one they'll use to log in), designation, and shift timings → click Add Staff Member.
3
The Attendance tab shows today's live records for all staff — updates in real time as staff check in/out from their phones.
4
Click any staff card in Manage Staff to open their full date-range history and export it as CSV.
👤 Staff (Housekeeping / Security / etc.)
1
Open this same link on your phone. Tap Sign in with Google — use the exact same email the admin entered for you when adding you as staff.
2
Your name, designation, and shift load automatically — no setup needed.
3
Tap ✅ Check In when you arrive. A confirmation popup shows the exact time — tap Confirm Entry to save it.
4
Tap 🏁 Check Out when you leave. If you go for lunch and come back, just tap Check In again — multiple entries per day are supported.
5
Scroll down to see your last 14 days of attendance. Tap ⬇ Export CSV to download your full personal attendance record.
🔘 What Every Button Does
🔑
Sign in with Google Admin & Staff
Opens Google's secure login screen. Admins can use any Google account. Staff must use the exact email the admin registered them with — otherwise they'll see "Account not recognised."
📝
Register Admin Admin only
Creates a new society office account using email & password. Each society registers separately — their data is completely private from other societies. Staff do NOT register here.
➕
Add Staff Member Admin only
Adds a new staff profile. The email field here is what that person must use to log in — make sure it matches their Google account email exactly. Once added, they can immediately sign in.
✏️
Edit Staff (pencil icon) Admin only
Changes a staff member's name, designation, or shift timings. Email cannot be edited (it's the login key) — to change email, delete and re-add the staff member.
🗑️
Delete Staff (bin icon) Admin only
Permanently removes that staff profile. Their past attendance records stay in the database but they can no longer log in to mark attendance.
👤 card
Click a Staff Card Admin only
Opens that staff member's full attendance history. Pick a "From" and "To" date → click Search (or just change the dates — it auto-searches). Use ⬇ Export to download their records as a CSV spreadsheet.
✅
Check In Staff only
Marks your arrival time. Opens a confirmation popup showing the exact current time — tap Confirm Entry to save. This also automatically emails the admin. You can check in again after a lunch break.
🏁
Check Out Staff only
Marks your departure time. Only becomes active after you've checked in. Opens a confirmation popup — tap Confirm Exit to save. Admin is notified automatically.
📊
Export CSV Admin & Staff
Admin — Today's records: exports all staff's attendance for today in one spreadsheet.
Admin — Staff history: exports one staff member's records for the selected date range.
Staff: exports your complete personal attendance history — use this as proof of your attendance.
🔍
Search (date filter) Admin only
Filters a staff member's history to the chosen date range. Also triggers automatically when you change either date picker — no need to click Search manually.
🔒
Forgot Password Admin only
Sends a password reset link to the admin's email. Works only for email/password accounts — Google Sign-In accounts are managed by Google directly.
↩️
Sign Out Admin & Staff
Securely logs out the current user. The next person to open the app will see the login screen fresh — important on shared devices.
📧 How Emails Work
✅ Check-In Notification
Sent automatically to the admin's registered email the moment a staff member confirms their check-in. Contains the staff member's name, email, check-in time, and date. The admin does not need to do anything — the email just arrives in their inbox.
🏁 Check-Out Notification
Same as above but for check-out. Also sent automatically to the admin on confirmation. If a staff member checks in and out multiple times (e.g. lunch break), an email is sent for each event.
🔒 Password Reset Email
Sent by Firebase (Google) directly to the admin's registered email when "Forgot password?" is clicked. Contains a secure link to set a new password. Valid for 1 hour.
⚠️ If Staff Show "Account Not Recognised"
This means the email they signed in with (their Google account email) does not match what the admin typed when adding them. Ask the admin to check the email in Manage Staff and correct it — or the staff member should try a different Google account that matches the registered email.
🔄 Multiple Check-Ins Per Day
🍽️
Lunch Breaks & Return Visits
Staff can check in and out as many times as needed in one day. Example: arrive at 9 AM → check in. Leave for lunch at 1 PM → check out. Return at 2 PM → check in again. Leave at 6 PM → check out again.

The app stores all entry pairs (9–1, 2–6). Total hours adds them together (8h 00m). The admin's attendance table shows all pairs for the day. Each pair appears on its own row in the CSV export with an Entry# column.
🔐 Privacy & Data
🏢
Each Society's Data is Isolated
When you register as a new admin (e.g. "Ratna Apt" and "Mansi Residency"), each society's staff and attendance are completely separate. One society can never see another's records.
☁️
Data is Stored in Firebase (Google Cloud)
All attendance records, staff profiles, and admin accounts are stored securely in Google Firestore. Data persists permanently — closing or refreshing the browser does not lose any records. Historical records are available forever.
