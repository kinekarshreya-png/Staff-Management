📖 How to Use This App

Society Attendance Portal — complete guide for admins and staff

👥 Two Roles — One App
🏢 Admin (Society Office)
1
Go to Register Admin tab → enter your society name, email and password → create your account. Do this only once.
2
After logging in, click Manage Staff tab → fill in each staff member's name, their email address, designation, and shift times → click Add Staff Member.
3
The Attendance tab shows all staff check-ins for today in real time. Summary cards at the top show Present / Late / Absent counts.
4
Click any staff card in Manage Staff to open their full attendance history. Pick a date range, view all entries, and export to CSV.
👤 Staff (Housekeeping / Security / etc.)
1
Open this same link on your phone. Tap Sign in with Google using the exact email your admin registered you with. Do not create a new account.
2
Your name, designation, and shift load automatically. The society name appears at the top.
3
Tap ✅ Check In when you arrive → a popup shows the exact time → tap Confirm Entry to save it.
4
Tap 🏁 Check Out when you leave. If you come back after lunch, tap Check In again — multiple entries per day are supported.
5
Your full attendance history is below the buttons. Tap ⬇ Export CSV to download it as proof.
🔘 What Every Button Does
🔑
Sign in with GoogleBoth
Opens Google's secure login. Admins use any Google account. Staff must use the email the admin registered them with — any other email shows "Account not recognised."
📝
Register AdminAdmin only
Creates a new society office account with email + password. Each society registers separately — data is 100% private. Staff do not register here.
➕
Add Staff MemberAdmin only
Adds a staff profile. The email you enter here is the login key for that staff member — it must exactly match their Google account email. Once added, they can sign in immediately.
✏️
Edit Staff (pencil icon)Admin only
Change a staff member's name, designation, or shift. Email cannot be edited (it's the login identity) — to change email, delete and re-add the staff member.
🗑️
Delete Staff (bin icon)Admin only
Removes the staff profile permanently. The person can no longer log in. Past attendance records remain in the database.
👤
Click a Staff CardAdmin only
Opens that person's full attendance history. Select From and To dates → click Search (or just change the dates — it auto-searches). Export downloads a CSV with every entry-pair as a separate row.
✅
Check InStaff only
Marks arrival. Opens a confirmation popup showing the exact time — tap Confirm Entry. Saves to the database and auto-emails the admin. Available again after each check-out (for multiple entries per day).
🏁
Check OutStaff only
Marks departure. Only active after checking in. Tap Confirm Exit. Admin is notified automatically. After checkout, Check In becomes available again.
⬇️
Export CSVBoth
Admin (Attendance tab): exports all staff for today. Admin (staff card popup): exports one staff member's selected date range. Staff: exports your complete personal history — use as proof of attendance.
🔒
Forgot PasswordAdmin only
Sends a reset link to the admin's email. Works only for email/password accounts. Google Sign-In passwords are managed by Google.
↩️
Sign OutBoth
Securely logs out. Important on shared devices — the next person will see the login screen fresh.
📧 How Emails Work
✅ Check-In / 🏁 Check-Out Notifications
Sent automatically to the admin's registered email every time a staff member confirms an entry or exit. No manual step needed. Contains staff name, action (Checked IN / OUT), exact time, and date.
🔒 Password Reset
Sent by Firebase (Google) when "Forgot password?" is clicked. Valid for 1 hour. Only works for email/password accounts.
⚠️ "Account not recognised" — What it means
The email the staff member signed in with does not match what the admin registered. Fix: admin goes to Manage Staff, checks the email on that staff card, and corrects it. Or the staff member should sign in with a Google account that matches the registered email.
🔄 Multiple Check-Ins Per Day
🍽️
Lunch Breaks & Return Visits
Staff can check in and out any number of times in one day. Example: arrive 9 AM → check in. Leave for lunch 1 PM → check out. Return 2 PM → check in again. Leave 6 PM → check out.

All pairs (9–1 PM and 2–6 PM) are stored. Total hours = 8h 00m. The attendance table and CSV show each pair numbered (1. 9:00 AM → 1:00 PM | 2. 2:00 PM → 6:00 PM).
🔐 Privacy & Data
🏢
Each Society's Data is Completely Isolated
Ratna Apt and Mansi Residency (for example) each have their own admin account. Their staff lists and attendance records are entirely separate — no society can ever see another's data.
☁️
Stored Permanently in Google Firebase
All data lives in Google Firestore (cloud database). Closing the browser, refreshing, or switching devices never loses records. History goes back as far as the first check-in ever recorded.
